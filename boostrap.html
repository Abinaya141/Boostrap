Top 10 Tourist Places in Kerala

Munnar

Munnar is known for its tea plantations, misty hills, and scenic landscapes.

Explore Munnar
Alleppey

Alleppey is famous for its beautiful backwaters and houseboat cruises.

Backwaters of Alleppey
Kovalam

Kovalam offers one of the best beaches in India, with pristine sands and gentle waves.

Kovalam Beach
Thekkady

Thekkady is known for its wildlife sanctuary and exotic flora and fauna.

Explore Thekkady
Wayanad

Wayanad is famous for its waterfalls, caves, and spice plantations.

Discover Wayanad
Varkala

Varkala is a seaside destination known for its cliffs and serene beaches.

Varkala Cliff Beach
Kochi

Kochi, also known as Cochin, is a vibrant city with a rich colonial history.

Visit Kochi
Vythiri

Vythiri is a hill station known for its forests, wildlife, and adventure activities.

Explore Vythiri
Kumarakom

Kumarakom is a tranquil village on Vembanad Lake, known for its backwaters and bird sanctuary.

Kumarakom Backwaters
Bekal
Bekal is famous for its historic fort, which offers stunning views of the Arabian Sea.
Explore Bekal Fort
